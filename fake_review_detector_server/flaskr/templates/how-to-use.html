How it works?

 

At Smartacus we are investigating hotel, restaurants and cafe reviews– but our methods don’t rely on gut feelings. We leverage algorithms and data science and have the ability to scan thousands of reviews and identify unnatural patterns in just seconds.

Here’s a bit more about how our system works:

First, we gather review data.

We gather publicly available review data from platforms such as Tripadvisor and Yelp. Not only are we collecting the set of reviews for a given hotel or restaurant, we categorize them into fake or real. Even the most critical readers will not likely spend hours tallying up statistics on every review for a hotel or restaurant. At Smartacus, we automatically check all available data to give you a complete picture of what’s really going on.

Next, we run the data through our analyzer.

Once we have all the data about a hotel or restaurant, we perform an analysis and run multiple different tests. We’re looking at every single hotel or restaurant in the same way – we don’t do any favours for anyone or make any individual judgement calls. We use statistical modelling on any suspicious patterns we detect in order to rule out the possibility they are due to random chance.

Last, we display a report that any human can easily understand.
