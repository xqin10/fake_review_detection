Our Team

Smartacus' goal is to eliminate fraud and fake reviews in the hospitality industry, bringing confidence and openness to the Internet. Smartacus protects its users while also saving them time and money by detecting fake reviews in real-time using artificial intelligence. Using our technology, we can easily detect suspicious behaviour and assist users in making rational choices by analysing billions of user reviews. We were fed up with being tricked online and decided to make it our job to ensure that this never happens to someone else.
