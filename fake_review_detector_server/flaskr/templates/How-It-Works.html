Our Proces​s

Step 1

Gather Review Data

We gather publicly available review data from platforms such as Tripadvisor and Yelp. Not only are we collecting the set of reviews for a given hotel, restaurant, we categorize them into fake or real.


Step 2

Run Through Analyzer

Once we have all the data about a hotel or restaurant, we perform an analysis and run multiple different tests. We’re looking at every single hotel or restaurant in the same way – we don’t do any favours for anyone or make any individual judgement calls.

Step 3

Display Report

We present our findings in a transparent and easy to understand format that helps you see how we arrived at our conclusions.




Even the most critical readers will not likely spend hours tallying up statistics on every review for a hotel or restaurant. At Smartacus, we automatically check all available data to give you a complete picture of what’s really going on. We use statistical modelling on any suspicious patterns we detect in order to rule out the possibility they are due to random chance.
